About Us

A dessert brand built for everyday celebrations

Pitch Parfait started with a simple idea: when life feels loud, a small sweet moment can reset everything. We craft parfaits, swirls, shakes, and mini cakes that look as good as they taste—soft, pink, and unapologetically joyful.

Mission

Make dessert feel effortless: consistently delicious, beautifully presented, and easy to order—so every craving finds a happy place.

Vision

Become the most-loved pink dessert stop in the city—where flavor, comfort, and celebration meet in every cup.

What we believe
  • Soft flavors, clean ingredients, and clear nutrition info.
  • Consistent quality—every order should feel familiar and special.
  • Design matters: dessert is a mood, not just a menu item.
